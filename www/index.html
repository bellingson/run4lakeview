Race Information

The race starts in front of Hockeyville USA 2016 winner: Lakeview arena. The course heads along the bike path passing the beautiful picnic rocks continuing down past McCarty’s cove, the Historic lighthouse with the turn around just prior to Mattson lower harbor park. After the turn runners will retrace their steps to picnic rocks. Then continue along bike path, paralleling Lakeshore Blvd, crossing Pine street and taking the bike path back to the finish at Lakeview Arena.

Kids 1-Mile Race

Starts in front of Lakeview going to Lakeshore Blvd taking a sharp left and then following the end of the 5K finish.

All kids receive a finishers award.

Following the racing events there will be the Sound Physicians post race celebration who has teamed up with Radio Results Networks, 94.9 The Bay, to have live music at the event. There will be lots of activities for all to enjoy, including but not limited to, street hockey, a skill competition, food and merchandise vendors, fire trucks on display, and activities for the kids. We will have dozens of silent auction items and raffle basket draws. This event will also be the first NHL preseason ticket pickup if you won a set in the lottery! Come down to support the racers and Lakeview Arena Saturday October 1st 2016. Post race party starts goes from 1030a-130p. Raffle baskets will be drawn at 1pm.

Run4Lakeview 5K Course

The 5K start and finish is in front of Lakeview Arena. Start by running toward the lake on Fair Ave, cross Lakeshore Drive to the bike path and head south towards the Coast Guard station. Shortly after the passing the Coast Guard Station, turn around and head back toward Lakeview. Continue past Fair Ave to the intersection of Pine and Lakeshore Drive. Turn left and follow the bike path along Pine Street back to the finish at Lakeview Arena.

Since this is an Out-and-Back course, please stay to the right side of the path to allow racers a clear path.
